exchanges

For hodlers who want to buy, miners who want to sell and everyone in between the exchanges are the liquidity watering hole where all market participants meet.

centralized decentralized

recommendations

the best practices
  • The MWC Team does not endorse any particular exchange and strongly recommends withdrawing any MWC to your personal wallet.
  • For real-time metrics visit:  MWC Dashboard
  • Updated statistics on the MWC trading activity will be kept on many data aggregator sites like  CoinMarketCap ,  CoinGecko ,  Nomics  etc.
  • Exchanges provide an important role for MWC because that is where liquidity can be sought and price discovery can happen.
  • Therefore, the MWC Team wants MWC to be listed on as many exchanges as possible. The more choices the better.  At the bottom of the page , please ask your favorite exchange to support the trading of MWC.
