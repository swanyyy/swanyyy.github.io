recommendations

the best practices
  • Verify the signature and hash of the release you download, the guide can be found here.
  • Always back-up your seed phrase in a safe place, and keep it condidential.
  • Use your own node on your wallet.
  • Make sure if you download a new node release that it follows your conscensus
  • You can follow the MWC guide to learn how to use your Desktop, Mobile or node and cli-wallet,
