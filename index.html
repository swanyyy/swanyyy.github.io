MWC

Extremely Scarce Ghost Money

With a provable supply cap of 20,000,000 MWC. MimbleWimbleCoin is a scarce pure proof of work Mimblewimble based coin that enables greater network scalability, privacy, and fungibility than legacy blockchain protocols.

All transactions on the base layer use Greg Maxwell's Coin Join with his Confidential Transactions and signature aggregation.

Non-Interactive Transaction

To improve the easy of use, the common user experience and further consolidate the privacy of MWC, we are integrating both one-sided payment (non-interactive transaction, or realtime off-line transaction) feature and the stealth address feature. MWC users will get an address for the wallet, which enable the coin receiving by publishing this stealth address to the sender, in the same time, the published transaction data will never disclose this address info for other people.
